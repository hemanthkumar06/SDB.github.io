ELASTOMERIC BRIDGE BEARINGASPER IRC 83 2015(Part-2)/2018

An elastomeric bridge bearing is a commonly used modern bridge bearing. ... Elastomeric bearing pads compress on vertical load and accommodate horizontal rotation and provide lateral shear movement. Elastomeric bearing pads are the most economical solution used in construction of large span bridges and buildings.

The chemical process for rubber or related to polymer into more durable material by heating them with sulfur is more percentage to produce an unreinforced elastomeric bearing.
